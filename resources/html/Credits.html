Inspiration

Inspiration for BBTag is coming from imglab tool in dlib (Davis King). The idea behind BBTag was to provide more options and flexibility for labeling images as well as being easily portable across platform (thanks to Qt).


Special thanks

Last but not least, many thanks to Adrian Rosebrock and his Python tutorials + courses through PyImageSearch, which gave me the motivation for this little project.


Author

I'm nobody with a little experience in Qt and C++ and I just figured this tool could be helpful for intensive tagging of images for machine learning purposes (it is at least to me :P). Please don't hesitate to report issues and/or suggestions for improvements on the BBTag project GitHub page.
